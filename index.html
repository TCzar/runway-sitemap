8. Link Confidence and App-Route Note

Public Runway website, Help Center, Academy, pricing, changelog, Watch, and product links are intended as verified public destinations whose visible page content matches the listed subject. Links to app.runwayml.com are publicly reachable internal app routes, but many signed-in destinations are JavaScript-gated, workspace-dependent, role-dependent, or rollout-dependent; therefore their exact in-app landing content cannot always be independently verified from public page content. For that reason, v12 avoids brittle workspace-specific routes such as team/guest paths and favors the generic app entry point when an internal deep link is not safely reusable. Entries without links are preserved as observed or useful navigation labels when no sufficiently confident URL was available.
